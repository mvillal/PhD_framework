# STORM

## 📋 Executive Summary
A system that automates the "pre-writing" phase of research by simulating a dialogue between a writer and a domain expert to discover diverse perspectives.

## 🛠️ Core Methodology
- **Writer-Expert Dialogue:** Generates a structured outline by asking probing questions from multiple "personas" before retrieving data.
- **Co-STORM (2025):** Collaborative version allowing human researchers to guide the agent in real-time.
