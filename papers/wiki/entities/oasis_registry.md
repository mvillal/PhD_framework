# OASIS Registry (Ophthalmic Analysis System & Information Services)

## 📋 Overview
The OASIS Registry is a large-scale, longitudinal ophthalmic dataset maintained by the Singapore National Eye Centre (SNEC). It contains comprehensive clinical data on uveitis patients, providing a robust resource for long-term outcome prediction and risk analysis.

## 🔬 Role in Research
- **Recurrence Risk Prediction:** Served as the data foundation for building predictive models aimed at quantifying the risk of uveitis recurrence over time [[Agrawal et al. (2025)](../../sources/uveitis/agrawal_2025_oasis_uveitis_recurrence.md)].
- **Clinical Decision Support:** Enables the development of tools to assist clinicians in long-term patient management and treatment planning for inflammatory eye diseases.

## 📊 High-Signal Metrics & Characteristics
- **Scope:** Large-scale longitudinal cohort of uveitis patients from Singapore.
- **Application:** Used for fine-tuning predictive models to identify patients at high risk of relapse based on historical clinical features [[Agrawal et al. (2025)](../../sources/uveitis/agrawal_2025_oasis_uveitis_recurrence.md)].
