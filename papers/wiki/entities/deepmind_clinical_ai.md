# DeepMind Clinical AI

## 🏢 Overview
DeepMind's Clinical AI group is at the forefront of building the next generation of medical foundation models and agentic AI systems. They are known for their high-performance models like **Med-PaLM 2**, **Med-Gemini**, and **AMIE**.

## 🛠️ Key Technologies
- **Med-Gemini (2024):** A natively multimodal clinical foundation model that uses **Uncertainty-Guided Search** to ground its reasoning in medical literature [[Saab et al. (2024)](../../sources/deepmind/saab_2024_med_gemini.md)].
- **AMIE (2024):** An agentic AI system designed for multi-turn diagnostic dialogue and clinical reasoning [[Tu et al. (2024)](../../sources/deepmind/tu_2024_amie_agentic_ai.md)].
- **AlphaFold Integration:** Research into how protein structure prediction can be integrated with clinical AI for drug discovery and personalized medicine.

## 🩺 Clinical Impact
By pushing the boundaries of multimodal and agentic AI, DeepMind aims to provide clinicians with "super-human" diagnostic and reasoning assistants that can process vast amounts of medical knowledge in real-time.
