# UCL / Moorfields Eye Hospital (UK)

## 📋 Overview
University College London (UCL) in partnership with Moorfields Eye Hospital represents one of the leading research centers globally for ophthalmic AI. They specialize in large-scale dataset integration and foundation model development within the UK National Health Service (NHS).

## 🔬 Role in Research
- **RETFound:** Developed the foundational model for ophthalmology, pre-trained on 1.6 million retinal images using self-supervised learning (SSL) [[Zhou et al. (2023)](../../sources/uveitis/zhou_2023_retfound_foundation_model.md)].
- **Data Integration:** Pioneered the use of large-scale NHS clinical imaging data for generalizable disease detection, including diabetic retinopathy and glaucoma.

## 📊 High-Signal Metrics & Characteristics
- **RETFound Training:** 1.6M unlabeled retinal images, Masked Autoencoder (MAE) architecture with 75% masking.
- **Focus:** Generalizability across imaging devices and patient populations, emphasizing rare disease detection with limited labeled data [[Zhou et al. (2023)](../../sources/uveitis/zhou_2023_retfound_foundation_model.md)].
