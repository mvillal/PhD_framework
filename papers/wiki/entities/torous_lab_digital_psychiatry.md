# Division of Digital Psychiatry (Beth Israel Deaconess Medical Center)

## 🏢 Overview
The Division of Digital Psychiatry, led by **John Torous**, focuses on the clinical implementation and validation of digital health tools in standard mental healthcare. They are known for their patient-centered approach and the development of "Digital Navigators."

## 🛠️ Key Platform: mindLAMP
**mindLAMP** (Learn, Assess, Manage, Prevent) is an open-source platform designed for clinical and research use. It emphasizes:
- **Direct-to-Clinician Dashboards:** Real-time data visualization for clinical decision support.
- **Patient Engagement:** Tools for self-management and cognitive training alongside monitoring.
- **Transparency:** Fully open-source codebase to ensure scientific reproducibility.

## 🔬 Core Research Focus
- **Clinical Implementation:** How to integrate digital tools into busy clinical workflows.
- **Digital Navigators:** A new healthcare role focused on helping patients and clinicians use digital health data effectively.
- **LLM-Augmented Care (2025-2026):** Using LLMs (e.g., GPT-4o) to interpret passive sensor data for real-time clinical insights.

## 🩺 Clinical Impact
The division bridges the gap between technical innovation and bedside care, ensuring that digital phenotyping leads to improved patient outcomes and clinician satisfaction.
