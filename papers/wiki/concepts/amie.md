# AMIE (Articulate Medical Intelligence Explorer)

## 📄 Definition
**AMIE** is an agentic AI system by Google DeepMind (2024) optimized for diagnostic reasoning and clinical communication. It is designed to act as a diagnostic partner capable of conducting structured patient interviews and gathering multi-turn history.

## 🛠️ Core Capabilities
- **Iterative History-Taking:** Unlike static models, AMIE asks follow-up questions to clarify symptoms, mimicking the structured approach of a human clinician.
- **Diagnostic Reasoning:** Uses an internal chain-of-thought process to generate and refine differential diagnoses as new information is gathered.
- **Empathic Communication:** Fine-tuned specifically for clinical "bedside manner," achieving high scores in patient empathy and clarity during simulated consultations.

## 🩺 Clinical Impact
In randomized simulations, AMIE demonstrated higher diagnostic accuracy and better communication quality scores than primary care physicians, suggesting a role in scalable triage and initial diagnostic screening.
