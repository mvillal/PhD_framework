# The Lancet Psychiatry - Clinical AI Guidelines

## 🩺 Reporting Standards
- **Mandatory Extensions:** Must adhere to **CONSORT-AI** (Trials) or **SPIRIT-AI** (Protocols).
- **Prediction Models:** Must adhere to **TRIPOD-AI**.

## 📝 Required Sections
- **Research in Context Panel:** A mandatory box with three sections:
    1. **Evidence before this study:** What was known before?
    2. **Added value of this study:** What does this study add?
    3. **Implications of all the available evidence:** How should this change practice?
- **Data Sharing Statement:** Detailed plan for de-identified data sharing.

## 🖋️ Writing Style & Tone
- **Focus:** Clinical utility, patient outcomes, safety, and interpretability.
- **Audience:** Clinicians and researchers. Avoid excessive technical jargon; explain ML concepts in clinical terms.
- **Ethics:** Deep focus on patient privacy, consent, and potential for automation bias.

## 🚫 AI Writing Restrictions
- **Authorship:** AI cannot be an author.
- **Disclosure:** Use of GenAI for writing must be disclosed in **Acknowledgments**.
- **Images:** AI-generated images/figures are strictly prohibited.
