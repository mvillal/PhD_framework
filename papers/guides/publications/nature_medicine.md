# Nature Medicine - Translational AI Guidelines

## 🧬 Core Requirements
- **Translational Impact:** The study must demonstrate clear potential for clinical translation or impact on human health.
- **Robustness:** High emphasis on multi-center validation or external validation cohorts.
- **Interpretability:** Models must provide actionable insights, not just high performance (Black-box models are discouraged without robust interpretability analysis).

## 🩺 Reporting Standards
- **Mandatory:** Adherence to **CONSORT-AI**, **STARD-AI**, or **TRIPOD-AI** depending on the study design.
- **Checklist:** Must complete the *Nature Portfolio Reporting Summary*.

## 🖋️ Writing Style & Tone
- **Focus:** Medical significance, biological/clinical mechanism, and practical implementation.
- **Tone:** Authoritative yet accessible to a broad medical and scientific audience.
- **Visuals:** Exceptionally high standards for data visualization. Figures must be clear, aesthetically polished, and medically intuitive.

## 🔒 Ethics & Data
- **Privacy:** Rigorous documentation of IRB approval and informed consent.
- **Availability:** Strong expectation for code and data availability to ensure reproducibility.
